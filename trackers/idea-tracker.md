## Ranking rubric
- `Novelty`: does it avoid already-saturated directions?
- `Tractability`: can you run a minimal probe without a giant new system?
- `Faithfulness`: does it improve actual temporal grounding or evidence use?
- `Applied leverage`: would the result matter in production-style video systems?
